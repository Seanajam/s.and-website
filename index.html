Artist Biography

Sean creates bespoke sand-cast jewelry that blends timeless craftsmanship with modern design. Each ring and pendant is cast in silver using the ancient art of sand casting, capturing organic textures that ensure no two pieces are ever alike. Inspired by the raw beauty of gemstones, Sean’s jewelry is bold, refined, and deeply individual—made for those who value originality and authentic artistry.
